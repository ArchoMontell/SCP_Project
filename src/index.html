About SCP Foundation

The SCP Foundation is a global organization responsible for securing, containing, and protecting anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena. Our storage facilities ensure safe containment and strict monitoring of all classified items stored within our secured chambers.

System Features

  • Management of all storage chambers
  • Monitoring of anomalous objects and containment status
  • Scheduled cleaning and technical maintenance
  • Access control and security supervision
  • Reports and analytics
